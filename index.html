Dainware

Creating Useful Apps

Committed to creating apps that people need, Dainware focuses on creating solutions to everyday problems. Newly founded in 2025, Dainware has already released ‘Echo’, a real time in-ear language translation tool, helping people all over the world understand the world around them. Next up, ‘Tip Snap’ – an AI assisted tip calculator, allowing users to calculate tips immediately with just the snap of a photo. Followed by ‘OnlyScans’ – Bringing augmented reality to everyone.
